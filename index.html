How It Works

A real fund. A real process. Built for people with day jobs.

01 — Joining

Members are admitted by application and referral. Each member contributes a $10K GP commit, your stake in the fund and your signal to the group. Members hold Class A status when actively contributing and Class B when taking a passive role.

02 — Sourcing

Deals come from the network. A member enters an opportunity into the Syn Syn system, which generates an overview and matches the sourcing member with the syndicate members best positioned to evaluate it based on expertise and proximity.

03 — Diligence

Matched members lead the initial work covering market, founder, traction, risks, and terms. If the diligence team develops conviction, they bring it to Investment Committee. If not, it's a clean pass with a memo on file.

04 — Investment Committee

IC is open to all syndicate members. The sourcing team presents, members ask questions and share signal, and the group votes. A quorum is required to make an investment, ensuring decisions reflect genuine collective conviction.

05 — Conflicts of Interest

Members with a material relationship to a company under review, as an employee, advisor, or existing investor, recuse from the vote. Logged and disclosed.

06 — Portfolio

Post-investment, members contribute through their networks and expertise: hiring, introductions, strategic input, and access to follow-on capital.
